Escape Room

Instructions:

Two or more players are locked into a room and are given two six-sided dice each. Each player will roll their dice at the same time and find the sum of their dice, then the security lock (our program), will generate a random number. If a player's sum matches the security door number, they are allowed to escape and win the game. Last player locked in loses!











The functionality of this program represents my ability to use inline and external CSS, library functions,
such as the random number generator (Math.random()) in JavaScript, and code/use conditional statements.
